CAMP Fund

For qualified investors, there is a rare opportunity to go on the offensive in what has begun as a highly defensive investment year. Campaign Advanced Media Purchase Fund, LLC (CAMP) is offering a unique investment vehicle that enables investors to access the projected $3.4 billion market for local television political advertising time during this election year.

Read more

Operating on the basis of historically verifiable price increases, CAMP will deploy investor capital early in the election season to purchase non-candidate advertising time (called ‘issue’ time) scheduled to air in late October in key U.S. presidential and senatorial media markets. CAMP will resell the time later in the election cycle at substantial mark-ups but discounted below market rates at the time of sale. CAMP will target Super PACs and other large buyers of issue time for resale of its media inventory.

Based upon the experience and research of members of CAMP Manager, prices for local television issue advertising time increase reliably through election cycles in even-numbered years, peaking shortly before the November election. Despite that issue advertising time can be purchased at substantially lower rates early in the election year, financial and strategic constraints keep many Buyers buying early.
